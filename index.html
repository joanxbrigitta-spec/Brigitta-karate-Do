Technique Library

Karate Moves
Demonstrated

From foundational stances to advanced kicks — study the art.

🦵
Kick
Roundhouse Kick
Mawashi Geri — 回し蹴り

One of karate's most powerful and versatile strikes. The hip rotates fully as the leg sweeps in a circular arc, driving the ball of the foot or instep into the target.

Intermediate Lower Body Kumite Essential
🦵
Kick
Front Kick
Mae Geri — 前蹴り

The first kick taught to beginners. Drive the knee up, then snap the foot forward, striking with the ball of the foot. Fast and direct.

Beginner Lower Body
👊
Punch
Reverse Punch
Gyaku Zuki — 逆突き

The backbone of karate striking. Delivered from a forward stance, the rear hand fires forward with full hip rotation for maximum power generation.

Beginner Upper Body Kumite Essential
🥋
Kata
Bassai Dai
披塞大 — "Penetrating a Fortress"

One of the most important Shotokan kata. 42 movements expressing powerful intent, directional changes, and deep stances with explosive kime.

Intermediate Kata 42 Moves
🛡️
Block
Rising Block
Age Uke — 上げ受け

Deflects downward attacks to the head. The forearm sweeps upward in a circular arc, protecting the face while loading a counter-strike.

Beginner Defense
🦵
Kick
Side Kick
Yoko Geri — 横蹴り

Delivers force laterally with the heel or blade of the foot. Excellent range and penetrating power when executed with correct hip alignment.

Intermediate Lower Body
🤚
Strike
Knife Hand Strike
Shuto Uchi — 手刀打ち

Strike with the outer edge of the open hand. Targeting the neck, temple, or collarbone. Precision and snap are more important than raw power here.

Intermediate Upper Body Kata Staple
🛡️
Block
Downward Block
Gedan Barai — 下段払い

Sweeps low attacks — kicks or leg strikes — away from the body. The first block taught in virtually every karate system. Foundation of all kata.
